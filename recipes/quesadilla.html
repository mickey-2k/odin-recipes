Quesadilla

Description

A quesadilla is a quick and easy meal for people that are on the go or are just lown on energy at the moment. It can be as complicated as you make it! Here we will be making a relatively simple version that uses left over carne asada skirt steak. This recipe will also be utilizing some simple salsa and avacado that has been premade and bought from the store. This is meant to be one of those recipes that gets you through the day when you just need to eat something fast and without much effort.
