Quesadilla

Description

A quesadilla is a quick and easy meal for people that are on the go or are just lown on energy at the moment. It can be as complicated as you make it! Here we will be making a relatively simple version that uses left over carne asada skirt steak. This recipe will also be utilizing some simple salsa and guacamole that has been premade and bought from the store. This is meant to be one of those recipes that gets you through the day when you just need to eat something fast and without much effort.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas (Corn or flour)
  • Cheese of your choice
  • Protein of choice, fully cooked
  • Premade guacamole
  • Premade salsa

Steps

  1. Take the tortillas and place on a comal on medium heat
  2. Add cheese to the tortillas, trying to get as much surface area covered as possible
  3. Re-heat the meat in the microwave or stovetop
  4. Add meat to the tortilla once the cheese has begun melting
  5. Place another tortilla on top (or fold in half if you don't want another)
  6. Plate the quesadilla and add salsa and guacamole onto the top or side. Enjoy!
