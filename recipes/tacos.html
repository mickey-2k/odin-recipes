Street Tacos

Description

These tacos are a staple food for many people throughout the USA. Many of the best places where these tacos can be purchased is in small shops found along the streets of Mexican communities and areas with high foot traffic. Tacos like these are delicious to eat alone or share with friends.

The following recipe will try to recreate the feeling that is brought about when visiting these establishments. Fresh ingredients are a must for this recipe and it cannot be stressed that besides the meat, what really makes the tacos are the toppings that you add to it. And don't forget to buy yourself a nice refreshing drink to wash down these bad boys after all the work you put in!

Ingredients

  • Skirt Steak
  • Lime
  • Cilantro
  • Radishes
  • Onion
  • Avacado
  • Chef Merito Carne Asada Seasoning
  • Corn Tortillas

Steps

  1. Heat up the grill and prepare the skirt steak by seasoning it on each side
  2. While you wait for the grill to heat, chop the onion and cilantro into small pieces
  3. Slice the radishes into small wedges and set aside
  4. Cut the lime into wedges and set aside
  5. Take the peel and pit out of the avacado and set aside
  6. Now that the grill has heated up, place each piece of skirt steak on the grill, making sure that the meat is cooked all the way through by turning constantly
  7. Once the meat has been fully cooked, set it aside to cool down for 3~5 minutes
  8. While the meat is cooling, place the tortillas on the grill and flip regulary to make sure they don't burn. Once they obtain brown spots, take them off and place aside
  9. Now that the meat has cooled, dice it into small chunks and plate it with the other ingredients
  10. Now grab a tortilla, add some meat and the toppings of your choice. Enjoy!
